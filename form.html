About School

Apeejay School, Nerul, Navi Mumbai was established under the aegis of Apeejay Education in the year 1986. Mrs Sushma Paul Berlia is the Chairperson of Apeejay Education, who led this School from its inception. The School caters to the requirements of Navi Mumbai - the city of the 21st Century with classes from Nursery to XII and has come a long way. It has provided an ideal platform for the physical, mental, spiritual and emotional growth of children. The Institution's belief is that ‘Learning is a holistic process and is all-pervasive’. In the pursuit of excellence in education, the School has a long-standing reputation in the field of education and has the ranking of one of the top schools not only in Navi Mumbai and Maharashtra state but also at the national level.
